Dress Code

This is a formal affair with a magical twist. We would like to encourage guests to dress in enchanting formal attire. Think deep blues, soft silvers, and starry accents to bring a touch of magic.

With lots of love for the little ones

To allow all guests to relax and enjoy the evening, we kindly request that children under 14 not attend. Please contact us if you'd like help arranging childcare—we'd love to make it easier for you to celebrate with us.

Ceremony

The ceremony will begin promptly at 5pm. Please arrive at least 30 minutes early to be seated.

We invite you to be fully present with us during our ceremony. Please silence and put away phones and cameras — we promise to share the professional photos soon after!
